About

A publication for the age of execution

Today’s Tech is an independent publication covering AI Agents, Agentic Commerce, and the next generation of software execution.

What we cover

We focus on the systems replacing traditional software: autonomous agents, execution-first interfaces, commerce driven by intent, and the infrastructure shaping the next phase of digital business.

Why we exist

Most publications report on product releases, funding rounds, and industry noise. Today’s Tech is built to track a deeper shift: software moving from tools people use to systems that act on their behalf.

Editorial thesis

Software is no longer defined by what it shows. It is defined by what it executes.

We believe the most important technology story of this decade is the shift from interfaces to outcomes. AI agents, agentic commerce, and execution systems are not side trends. They are the foundation of the next software era.

Who publishes Today’s Tech

Today’s Tech is edited under the pseudonym Orion Hale, an independent analyst focused on AI systems, digital commerce, and the collapse of traditional SaaS assumptions.
